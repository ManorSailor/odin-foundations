Summertime Mango Drink

Description:

Deliciously delicious mango drink recipe right at your disposal

Nutrition Facts:

Per Serving: 115 calories; protein 3.7g; carbohydrates 20.8g; fat 2.2g; cholesterol 8.1mg; sodium 43.3mg. Full Nutrition

Ingredients:

  • 2 mangoes, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 ⅔ cups milk

Steps:

  1. Combine mangoes, ice cubes, vanilla extract, and sugar in a blender; pour in milk. Blend until liquefied.

Steak Marinade

Description:

Deliciously delicious steak marinade recipe right at your disposal

Nutrition Facts:

Per Serving: 156 calories; protein 1.2g; carbohydrates 7.8g; fat 13.5g; sodium 1135.4mg. Full Nutrition

Ingredients:

  • 1/4th cup olive oil
  • 1/4th cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4th cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4th cup soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Mix olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, Dijon mustard, and garlic in a small bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Marinate steaks for at least 2 hours for best flavor.
